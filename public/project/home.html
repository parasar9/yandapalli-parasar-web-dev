Welcome !

BookShelf is a platform for us to share every piece of reading experience through network. Interaction with your friends, through recommendations, book reviews, reading groups and Q&As, encourages to read more and think more.

Learn More

Book Review

You will have access to book reviews and rating from other readers. It makes you feel easy to pick up a book and start reading. Plus, you can post your own book reviews and share your insight.

Progress Management

Users can manage their own reading progress. They are also able to form reading groups, show their progress and stimulate each other.

Interaction

Leader of a reading group can publish questions for group members to answer and discuss, which encourages members to read on a frequent basis.
